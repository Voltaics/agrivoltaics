# Privacy Policy for Agrivoltaics

**Last Updated:** February 2, 2026

## Overview
The Agrivoltaics application ("App") is designed to provide information and interactive features for the UC Vinovoltaics project. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

## Data We Collect

### 1. Location Data
- The App may request access to your device's location to provide location-specific information.
- Location data is only used to enhance user experience and is not stored on servers without your explicit consent.

### 2. Camera Access
- If you use features requiring camera access, the App will request permission.
- Images captured are processed locally on your device unless you explicitly choose to upload them.

### 3. Firebase / Analytics (if enabled)
- If the App uses Firebase, we collect:
  - App crashes and performance data
  - Usage analytics (which screens you visit, feature usage)
  - Authentication data (if you sign in with Google)
- This data is processed by Google according to [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### 4. Authentication
- If you sign in with Google or other authentication providers, we collect your email and profile name.
- Authentication is handled by third-party providers (Google, etc.) and subject to their privacy policies.

### 5. No Direct Server Storage
- The App does not directly collect or store personal data on Voltaics/UC servers unless explicitly documented in the App.
- Any server communication (e.g., to InfluxDB) is for sensor data or research purposes only.

## How We Use Your Data
- To improve App functionality and user experience.
- To diagnose technical issues and improve performance.
- To provide personalized features (with your consent).
- We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

## Your Rights
- You can deny location, camera, or other permissions at any time via your device settings.
- You can clear the App's data or uninstall it at any time.

## Third-Party Services
- **Firebase**: Google's analytics and crash reporting service. See [Firebase Privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).
- **Google Authentication**: See [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **InfluxDB** (if used): Time-series database for sensor data. See [InfluxDB Privacy](https://www.influxdata.com/influxdb-cloud-privacy-policy/).

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
